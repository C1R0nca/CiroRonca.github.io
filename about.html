Who I Am

My Story

Born in beautiful Salerno in 2003, I developed strong computer skills from an early age — long before I knew it would become my career. I was the go-to person for my family and neighbours whenever a PC needed fixing, and those small moments of helping others through technology left a lasting impression on me.

In high school, that curiosity turned creative: I built a detailed web page dedicated to US Salernitana 1919, covering the club's history, players, and live result updates. It was my first real taste of building something that others actually used.

At university, I worked on multiple group projects — designing, implementing, and presenting demos with full technical reports. The highlight was my bachelor's thesis, developed in collaboration with ACK company in Salerno: an application that lets users upload a photo and receive a detailed AI-generated description, built entirely with free, open-source tools.

Today, I am fully focused on my Master's, while keeping a close eye on the frontier of artificial intelligence and robotics — reading research papers, following new developments, and looking for ways to turn ideas into something real and useful.
